Danish football

A small country (5.9 million people) that consistently produces quality football. The Danish Superliga punches well above its weight.

FC Copenhagen

Founded 1992. Plays at Parken Stadium (38,065). 16 Danish Superliga titles, the most in the modern era. Famous for selling players at huge profits (Andreas Cornelius, Pierre-Emile Højbjerg, Simon Kjær).

FC Midtjylland

Founded 1999. Plays at MCH Arena (11,535). 4 Danish titles. Built using data analytics from the start — the first major football club to use statistical analysis for player recruitment.

The "Danish model"

Danish clubs develop players and sell them. 50% of every Danish Superliga matchday squad must be Danish-developed — a league rule that keeps talent in the academy pipeline.

Danish football legends

Michael Laudrup, Brian Laudrup, Peter Schmeichel, Nicklas Bendtner, Christian Eriksen, Kasper Schmeichel, Kasper Dolberg, Mikkel Damsgaard, Rasmus Højlund, Rasmus Nissen.
