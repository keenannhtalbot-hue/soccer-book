Uruguayan giants

Despite a population of 3.4 million, Uruguay has won 2 World Cups. Football is the country's religion.

Peñarol

Founded 1891. Plays at Estadio Campeón del Siglo (40,000). 5 Copa Libertadores titles. The most successful Uruguayan club internationally. Yellow and black stripes.

Nacional

Founded 1899. Plays at Gran Parque Central (34,000). 3 Copa Libertadores titles, plus 3 Intercontinental Cups. White with a blue sash. The Bolso.

The Uruguayan Superclásico

Peñarol vs Nacional is the world's oldest clásico beyond Britain — first played in 1900. The match is so intense that historically, the two clubs' fans couldn't even share Montevideo's football buses.

Uruguayan legend

Diego Forlán (Peñarol, Manchester United, Villarreal, Atlético Madrid) won the 2010 World Cup Golden Ball. He's the most famous Peñarol alumnus.
