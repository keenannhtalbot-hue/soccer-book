Old Firm & Scottish football

The most intense football derby in the world — religious, political, and cultural — Celtic vs Rangers, the Old Firm.

Celtic FC

Founded 1888. Plays at Celtic Park (60,411). 54 Scottish titles — most in the country. The green-and-white hoops, founded by Irish Marist Brothers, have always been associated with the Catholic / Irish community in Scotland.

Rangers FC

Founded 1872. Plays at Ibrox Stadium (50,817). 55 Scottish titles — the all-time record. Founded by a Scottish-Protestant lineage, traditionally associated with the Unionist community.

The Old Firm

The Old Firm derby has been played over 430 times since 1888. Rangers lead with ~165 wins; Celtic have ~150; ~115 draws. Goals per game: ~2.7.

Modern shift

Celtic has dominated Scottish football in recent years, winning 11 of the past 13 league titles. Rangers won the title in 2020/21 — their first in 10 years — but Celtic have since reclaimed dominance.
