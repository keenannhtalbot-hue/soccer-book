IX · THE PLAYERS

CM partnerships

A central midfield partnership is the engine of any team. The best pairs are complementary: a 6 + 8, a 6 + 10, or a double-6 pivot. Wrong pairings break teams more than any other error.

Three pairings

6 + 8 — the classic. Sitter + runner.
6 + 10 — destroyer + creator.
8 + 8 — twin engines. Used at peak Barca with Xavi + Iniesta.

Reference: Xavi + Iniesta

Barça's 2008–12 midfield — Xavi as regista, Iniesta as 8 arriving late — was the perfect regista + mezzala. Between them they won 4 Champions League titles.

The 6 + 8 partnership
Stat

A stable 6+8 midfield pair that have played 100+ matches together concede 8% fewer goals than rotating combos.
