IX · THE PLAYERS

Goal-scorer profiles

Goals win games. They are also the most measurable stat in football. Modern goal-scorer profiling uses xG maps, shot location, body part, and press-resistance to differentiate the strikers who score from those who don't.

Five scoring styles

The poacher. 60%+ of shots inside the 6-yard box. Kane, Immobile.

The long-range specialist. 30%+ of shots from outside the box. Bounou, Ward-Prowse.

The header. 30%+ headers, dominant at set pieces. Haaland, Giroud.

The counter-attacker. Converts transitions, holds the line. Vardy.

The complete finisher. Variety: 60% inside, 25% outside, 15% headers. Mbappé.

The benchmark

Kylian Mbappé 2023/24 — 44 goals in 47 games across all competitions. Distribution: 28% inside box, 22% outside box, 8% headers, 42% counter-attack finishes. The most complete scoring profile in world football.
