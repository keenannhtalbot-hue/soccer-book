African giants

The Egyptian Premier League and North African football — with the Cairo derby the largest in Africa.

Al Ahly

Founded 1907. Plays at Cairo International Stadium (75,000). 43 Egyptian Premier League titles (record), 11 CAF Champions League titles (also record). The most successful African club in history.

Zamalek SC

Founded 1911. Plays at Cairo International Stadium. 14 Egyptian Premier League titles, 5 CAF Champions League titles. Al Ahly's fierce Cairo rivals.

Cairo derby

One of the most-watched derbies in the world. 100,000+ fans regularly attend Cairo International Stadium for the match. It is the largest football derby by attendance in the world.

Mohamed Salah

Salah began at El Mokawloon before moving to Basel, Roma, and Liverpool. He's now Egypt's all-time top scorer and most-capped player. African Player of the Year 2017, 2018.
