IX · THE PLAYERS

The modern 10s

The number 10 shirt has survived 30 years of tactical upheaval. Today's 10s are more athletic than the 2000s version, but the core brief — receive between the lines, thread the through-ball — is the same.

Five variations

1. The creator. Pure passer. Bruno Fernandes at Manchester United, Martin Ødegaard at Arsenal.

2. The dribbler. Carries past midfielders; creates from ball-progression. Florian Wirtz.

3. The second striker. Closer to a 9. Lionel Messi 2014–23.

4. The left-footed specialist. Inverts to the right half-space to shoot. David Villa at Barcelona.

5. The defensive 10. Presses; tackles; creates. Mason Mount under Tuchel.

Statistically the best

Top 10s register more than 0.40 xA per 90 and contribute 0.25+ xG per 90. Sample: Martin Ødegaard 2023/24 — 8 goals, 10 assists in 31 PL matches.
