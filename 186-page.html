Atalanta BC

From the foothills of the Alps to the top of European football — Atalanta's rise is one of the great underdog stories of the modern game.

Founded 1907. Based in Bergamo, Lombardy. Plays at the Gewiss Stadium (21,300). Has spent most of its existence in Serie B, only seriously competing at the top since the 2016/17 season.

The Gasperini revolution

Gian Piero Gasperini arrived in 2016 and turned Atalanta into the highest-scoring team in Europe. Three consecutive Champions League qualifications (2019–21) — funded entirely by player sales, never spending more than €50m on a single player.
