Content creators

THE FAN AS PUBLISHER

Football creators work with a phone, editing software, a camera, or a notebook—and publish before the traditional post-match programme has finished.

The creator toolkit

  • Short-form video: one idea, one hook, one visual explanation.
  • Long-form video: tactics, scouting, club history, or a match story with sources.
  • Graphics: line-ups, shot maps, timelines, and transfer explainers.
  • Community: comments, newsletters, live rooms, and membership spaces.

Access and responsibility

Creators can widen the football conversation, but they also work around copyright, player privacy, stadium rules, and platform incentives. A viral clip is not automatically fair or complete.

One match, many screens

The creator economy multiplies the ways a fan can encounter football: instant reaction, tactical education, comedy, archive, and community all sit beside the official feed.

SOURCE CHECK

Add the missing context

When a clip is dramatic, ask: when was it filmed, what happened before it, and what does the original source actually show?
