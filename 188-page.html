Section XII

Belgian giants

Belgium produces talent out of all proportion to its size — and these two clubs are the engine.

Club Brugge

Founded 1891. Plays at Jan Breydel Stadium (29,000). 18 Belgian titles, 11 Belgian Cups. The country's most successful club historically. Famous for developing players for sale (Vanaken, Mechele).

RSC Anderlecht

Founded 1908. Plays at Lotto Park (21,500). 35 Belgian titles — the record. Famous Belgian academy: Kompany, Lukaku, Tielemans, Hazard brothers, Vincent Kompany.

The Belgian advantage

Belgium has produced 11 Ballon d'Or nominees in the past decade — more than France, more than Argentina. The Jupiler Pro League is a talent-factory for the Premier League and Bundesliga.

The Belgium model

A small league, smaller wages, but outstanding academy infrastructure. Players developed at 8–14 years old; sold at 18–22 for huge profits. It's the model for every "small" football nation.
