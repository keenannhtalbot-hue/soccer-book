Section XII

Istanbul & Turkish giants

Beyond the Istanbul three (Galatasaray, Fenerbahçe, Beşiktaş), Turkey's heartlands are full of passionate football.

Beşiktaş

Founded 1903. Plays at Tüpraş Stadium (42,590). 16 Turkish Süper Lig titles. Black and white stripes. The "Black Eagles" — a fierce following in the Bosphorus-side working-class neighbourhoods.

Trabzonspor

Founded 1967. Plays at Papara Park (40,782). 7 Turkish Süper Lig titles — the only non-Istanbul club to win the league since 1959. Won a league and cup double in 2022/23. Based on the Black Sea coast.

Turkish passion

Süper Lig average attendance: ~26,000 per match. Beşiktaş, Fenerbahçe, and Galatasaray regularly sell out 40k+ stadiums. Turkey has the world's 6th-largest football market by revenue.

The Karadeniz derby

Trabzonspor's regional rivalries include Rizespor and Samsunspor — Black Sea coast clubs. Their style is direct, physical, hard-running — northern grit.
