IX · THE PLAYERS

Striker archetypes

The striker is the most-studied, most-scouted and most-mythologised position in football. Modern football distinguishes at least five striker archetypes — each defined by where they start, what they do with the ball, and how they finish.

Five types

1. The poacher. Goal-side, in the box. Reaction specialist.

2. The target man. Holds the ball, brings others into play.

3. The complete 9. Scores and links. Lewandowski, Haaland.

4. The pressing forward. Leads the press first, scores second.

5. The false 9. A 10 wearing the 9 shirt. See page 122.

Reference: the modern 9

Erling Haaland's first Premier League season (2022/23) saw him score 36 goals — a record. He played the complete 9 role: hold-up, link, finish. The benchmark the next generation will chase.
