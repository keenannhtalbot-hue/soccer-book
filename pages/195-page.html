Brazilian giants

Brazil's Série A is the most-watched football league in the Americas.

Botafogo

Founded 1904. Plays at Estádio Nilton Santos (46,931). 2023 Copa Libertadores & Brasileirão champions — their first league title in 28 years.

Flamengo

Founded 1895. Plays at Maracanã (capacity shared). 8 Brasileirão titles, 3 Copa Libertadores. The most-supported club in Brazil with 40M+ fans.

São Paulo FC

Founded 1930. Plays at Morumbi (66,795). 6 Brasileirão, 3 Copa Libertadores. The only club to win the FIFA Club World Cup twice (1992, 1993).

O Clássico-Véu

Flamengo vs Vasco — Rio's working-class derby. Played 460+ times, Flamengo lead by ~150 wins. The Maracanã crowds for these matches have historically reached 180,000+.
