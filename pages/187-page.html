Austrian giants

The Austrian Bundesliga — dominated by Red Bull Salzburg for a decade, but with challengers rising.

Red Bull Salzburg

Founded 1933. Stands at the Red Bull Arena (30,188). The Red Bull takeover in 2005 transformed the club into Austria's dominant force — 14 consecutive league titles (2014–2024). Famous for developing stars: Sadio Mané, Naby Keïta, Erling Haaland.

SK Sturm Graz

Founded 1909. The Merkur Arena (15,200). Austria's other powerhouse — 3-time Bundesliga champion, reached the UEFA Cup semi-finals in 2000. The "Schwoazn" (blackies) are famous for their ultras.

Red Bull machine

Salzburg's model: identify young African and South American talent, develop them for 1–3 seasons, then sell to top European clubs. The club is profitable on player trading alone.
