MLS rivalries

Major League Soccer is now the 8th-largest sports league in the world by revenue — and growing.

LA Galaxy vs LAFC — El Tráfico

The hottest new rivalry in American football. Galaxy's 5 MLS Cups vs LAFC's 2022 title and 2022 Supporters' Shield. Average attendance for El Tráfico: 55,000+ at the Rose Bowl / BMO Stadium.

Sporting KC & regional rivalries

Sporting Kansas City are 2-time MLS Cup champions (2000, 2013). Their rivalries include St. Louis CITY SC (founded 2019), Real Salt Lake, and Houston Dynamo.

The Beckham Rule

After David Beckham signed for LA Galaxy in 2007, MLS adopted the Designated Player rule — letting clubs sign up to 3 players above the salary cap. Messi (2023), Suárez (2024), Mbappé (2024) followed.
