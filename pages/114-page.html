IX · THE PLAYERS

The defensive mid · "the 6"

The defensive midfielder — universally known as the 6 — is the screen in front of the back four. Wins the ball, recycles possession, and dictates the first tempo of the build-up.

Modern 6s are also expected to play through pressure lines and contribute to chance creation. The great No. 6s of the 2020s — Rodri, Casemiro, Kanté — are all-rounders, not just ball-winners.

The three phases

Defending. Sits in front of the CBs, intercepts the second ball, blocks the central lane.
Build-up. The first man the CB looks for — receives between the lines, plays forward.
Transitions. First press after losing the ball (the "6-second press").

Rodri in 2023/24

Manchester City's Rodri completed 94.5% of his Premier League passes — the highest of any outfield player in the division. He also recorded the most tackles + interceptions in the league top 8. That's a complete 6.
