⚽ THE SOCCER BOOK

225 pages of the modern game,
packed with information.

From the pitch you play on to the kit you wear to the stadium you fill — The Soccer Book is the definitive visual encyclopedia of modern football.

Every spread is built to be skimmed, scanned, and studied in any order. Twelve numbered sections take you from the equipment and rules, through formations, kits, the world's biggest clubs, the stadiums and competitions, into the modern fan culture, and finally the tactics and player profiles that make the game tick.

Pitch diagrams with dotted-leader labels. Sidebars, factboxes, and timelines. Stat blocks, comparison tables, kit displays. Everything is cross-referenced, dated, and visually rich.

Whether you're a ten-year-old just learning what offside means, a parent trying to figure out why your kid screams at the TV every Saturday, or a serious fan who wants every stat at a glance — this is the book.
