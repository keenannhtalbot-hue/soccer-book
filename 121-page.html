IX · THE PLAYERS

The classic Number 10

The classic 10 — sometimes trequartista in Italy, playmaker in Spain, enganche in Argentina — is the between-the-lines creator. They play with their back to goal, between the opposition's midfield and defence.

Maradona, Zidane, Rivaldo, Ronaldinho, Özil — all wore the 10 shirt. The role has shrunk as false 9s and double 10s have spread, but the specialist is back in football.

The brief

1. Receive with back to goal, between the lines.

2. Turn or lay off in one touch.

3. Thread the through-ball or the disguised pass.

4. Score from deep positions.

Modern 10s

Bruno Fernandes at Manchester United, Martin Ødegaard at Arsenal, Florian Wirtz at Bayer Leverkusen 2023/24. The 10 lives.
