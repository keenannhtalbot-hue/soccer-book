Section XII

National teams

Six national teams that define the modern game — winners, innovators, and global superpowers.

Brazil — 5 stars

The only team to play every World Cup. Yellow and blue, samba style. Most goals in World Cup history (254). Last title: 2002.

Germany — 4 stars

Efficient, disciplined, machine-like. 2014 World Cup winner. Last title: 2014.

Argentina — 3 stars

2022 World Cup winner. Messi-led generation. Last title: 2022.

France — 2 stars

2018 World Cup + 2021 Nations League winner. Last title: 2018.

Spain — 1 star

2010 World Cup, 2008 & 2012 Euro winner. Tiki-taka era.

England — 1 star

1966 World Cup winner. Reaching Euro 2024 final (lost to Spain). Most-improved team of the decade.

Most caps

Cristiano Ronaldo (Portugal) holds the all-time record for men's international goals (132) and caps (213). On the women's side, Christine Sinclair (Canada) scored 190 international goals.
