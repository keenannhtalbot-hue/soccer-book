IX · THE PLAYERS

Midfield balance

A balanced midfield has both an anchor (6/regista) and a runner (8/10). Too many 6s makes a team risk-averse; too many 10s makes it porous in transition.

The balance formula

Modern formations split the midfield into three phases:

Build phase. Deep-lying 6 + box-to-box 8.

Create phase. Half-space 8 + half-space 10.

Defensive phase. The 6 screens; the 8 presses; the 10 drops onto the opposition 6.

Inverted-FB era

With two iFBs tucking in, a nominal 4-3-3 becomes a 2-3-5 in possession. The lone 6 must be elite on the ball — Rodri, Casemiro, Tchouaméni are the prototypes.
